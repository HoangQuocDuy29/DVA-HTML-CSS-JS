Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What age groups do you accept for training?

We welcome players of all ages from 8 to 60+ years old. Our programs are designed for different age groups and skill levels, ensuring everyone receives age-appropriate training.

Do I need to bring my own equipment?

For training sessions, we provide all necessary equipment including volleyballs, nets, and training aids. For competitive play, we recommend bringing your own knee pads and athletic shoes.

What are your payment options?

We accept cash, bank transfers, credit cards, and monthly payment plans. We also offer family discounts and early bird pricing for seasonal programs.

Can I try a class before committing?

Absolutely! We offer free trial sessions for all new students. This allows you to experience our training style and meet our coaches before making a commitment.

How do I join a competitive team?

Team selection is based on skill assessment, training dedication, and tryout performance. We hold tryouts twice a year and also scout from our training programs.
